# Community & Forum

The MICRORACK community is a vibrant space for dreamers, makers, performers, and geeks. Whether you're looking for inspiration, troubleshooting help, or a place to showcase your latest creation, our forum is the heart of the ecosystem.

## Join the Conversation

Visit the [MICRORACK Forum](https://forum.microrack.org) to:
- **Showcase Your Rig:** Share photos and videos of your setup.
- **Patch Sharing:** Post your favorite patches and learn from others.
- **Feature Requests:** Have an idea for a new module? Tell us!
- **Troubleshooting:** Get help from the community and the MICRORACK team.
- **Marketplace:** Buy, sell, or trade modules with other enthusiasts.

## For the Dreamers & Geeks
We love deep dives into synthesis theory, circuit design, and experimental music. If you're building your own modules or pushing the limits of the platform, you'll find your tribe here.

## For the Commenters & Fans
Your feedback drives our development. Join the discussions on upcoming releases, collaborations, and events like Superbooth.

## For the Haters (and Skeptics)
We value critical feedback! If something isn't working for you, or you think we can do better, start a thread. We're committed to "radical transparency" and continuous improvement.

---

### Community Guidelines
- **Be Kind:** We're all here to learn and create.
- **Stay On Topic:** Keep discussions relevant to MICRORACK and modular synthesis.
- **Share Freely:** MICRORACK is an open platform. Share your knowledge and help others grow.
